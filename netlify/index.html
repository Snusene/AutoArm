AutoArm Weapon Scoring System

This page lets you visualize how AutoArm evaluates weapons. The higher the score, the more likely a colonist is to upgrade to that weapon. Scores are based on:

  • DPS (Damage Per Second): Core weapon effectiveness
  • Range: Kiting potential and tactical flexibility (includes distance penalties)
  • Burst Fire: Multiple shots with diminishing returns
  • Armor Penetration: Effectiveness against late-game threats
  • Skill Preferences: Colonist shooting vs melee skills
  • Power Creep Protection: Extreme DPS (>30) has diminishing returns to prevent modded weapons from breaking balance
  • Situational Weapons: Grenades, launchers, and utility weapons are capped at 80 points to ensure they're only chosen for specific tasks

Developer note: I use this when fine-tuning AutoArm's weapon scoring balance. I had way too much fun styling this page.

About this system: Since vanilla RimWorld lacks automatic weapon equipping and therefore has no weapon evaluation formula to reference, AutoArm creates its own scoring system from scratch. No formula is perfect for every playstyle, but this aims to work well for most situations while also trying to respect modded behaviors (e.g., "Hunters Use Melee" mods).

Performance note: To keep AutoArm fast even with hundreds of modded weapons, the scoring system focuses only on these core factors.

Current dev notes

  • Situational weapons (grenades, launchers) are capped at 80 points to ensure they're only used when needed
  • Ranged multiplier: 10, Melee multiplier: 8 - slightly favors ranged weapons in balanced scoring
  • Range modifiers (simplified): 0.30x (<10), 0.40x (10-13), 0.55x (14-17), 0.90x (18-21), 0.95x (22-24), 1.0x (25-34), 1.02x (35+)
  • Range bonuses: +20 (35+), +15 (30-34), +10 (25-29), +5 (22-24), +2 (18-21), +0 (<18)
  • Burst bonus uses logarithmic scaling: 5 × log(burst + 1) - provides diminishing returns for high burst weapons
  • Power creep protection: DPS > 30 has diminishing returns (50% value for excess)
  • Quality affects both DPS and armor penetration values
  • Armor penetration thresholds: 100pts (≥0.75), 70pts (≥0.52), 50pts (≥0.40), 30pts (≥0.20), linear scale below
  • Skill scores use gradual exponential growth: Base 30 × 1.15^(skillDifference-1)
  • Brawler trait: +200 bonus for melee weapons
  • Hunting work: +100 bonus for all ranged weapons
  • Natural weapons (Thrumbo Horn, Alpha Thrumbo Horn, Elephant Tusk, Mastodon Tusk) have fixed stats that don't change with quality
