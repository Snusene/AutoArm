# Auto Arm

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Snusene/AutoArm/develop/About/preview.png)

Automatic weapon management mod for RimWorld 1.5/1.6.

## Features

- Pawns automatically equip weapons based on your Apparel Policy
- Respects ideology restrictions, traits, and skill levels
- Right-click any weapon to force-equip and pause automation
- Compatible with Combat Extended, Simple Sidearms, and more

## Requirements

- [Harmony](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2009463077)

## Installation

Subscribe on Steam Workshop or download and place in your RimWorld Mods folder.
